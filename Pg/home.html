What is XR?

XR, for "eXtended Reality" is a newly emerging catch all term for Virtual, Augmented and Mixed Reality technologies. As the relationship between humans and machines becomes increasingly collaborative, we believe that XR technologies will provide a platform by which our perception of, and interaction with, digital objects becomes grounded in our physical reality. To mitigate the issues arrising with XR market fragmentation, we follow the principles of web deployablity and wide interoperability as set by the Khronos OpenXR workgroup.

XR @ PROCESSGOLD

At Process Gold, we started on the road to XR through VR, as VR technologies, as of 2018, are more mature. Starting our journey with VR is beneficial as it allows us to immediatly explore the design problems accosiated with immersive analytics without hardware being too great a bottleneck. As the worlds of AR and MR evolve, we hope to investigate these channels also; with the long term goal of integrating ProcessGold:XR into our product lineup.

Demo

Feel free to explore our demo! On desktop, you can walk around using the WASD keys and you can query nodes by clicking while the cursor (black circle) is over them. If using a VR headset, enter the scene by using your controller to click on the VR goggles in the bottom right. The demo will also work on mobile, but you will not be able to navigate the scene.
